Electron IPC Benchmark

Measure messaging costs across Electron communication paths

This app compares round-trip latency for synchronous IPC, async IPC, invoke-based request-response, renderer relays, MessagePorts, and in-process frame messaging. Results are reported as total milliseconds for each batch size.
